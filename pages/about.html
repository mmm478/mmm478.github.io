ABOUT DOUGHBOY

Doughboy Desserts is a culinary start-up currently located in Miami, Florida. Founder & CEO, Zachary Sasportas, found his passion for baking and creativity during his first year of college. Zack thought he wanted to go corporate, but as he started to create his own treats from boxes purchased in the grocery store, he realized that baking was his passion and that he wanted to share that with the world. From there, Doughboy Desserts was born! Through testing recipes out in the kitchen, to decorating aesthetic desserts, there's nothing like DB in Miami.

Zack graduated from Florida International University'f Hospitality Manangement program in Fall of 2020. Through his college career, he learned from some of the best and brightest in the industry. He is SafeServ certified and is constantly taking courses to evolve his craft. After being in business for six months,Doughboy has reached customers all over Miami-Dade and Broward County.
